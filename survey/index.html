About you

Pillar 1 · Self-custody

Do you support the right of New Hampshire residents to hold and transact in digital assets, including Bitcoin, in self-custody — without state-level licensing, surveillance, or seizure beyond what existing law permits for cash and personal property?

Pillar 2 · Zero state taxation of digital-asset gains

New Hampshire has no general income tax, no sales tax, and no capital gains tax. Will you oppose any new state-level tax on the sale, exchange, or use of digital assets?

Pillar 3 · Opposition to CBDCs

Will you oppose any state-level adoption, integration, or use of a U.S. central bank digital currency (CBDC), and will you support legislation that prohibits state agencies from accepting or transacting in a CBDC?

Pillar 4 · The right to mine and run nodes

Will you defend the right of New Hampshire residents to operate Bitcoin miners and full nodes — including against energy rationing, zoning bans, or utility surcharges that target this activity specifically?

Pillar 5 · Encryption & digital privacy

Will you support strong encryption protections for New Hampshire residents — including opposition to mandatory backdoors, expansive device-search authority, and warrantless data collection by state agencies?

Pillar 6 · Smart-contract recognition

Will you support recognition of smart contracts and blockchain-based records under New Hampshire contract and evidence law, consistent with the existing UETA framework?
