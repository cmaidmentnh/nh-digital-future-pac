The Thesis

Freedom is infrastructure.

Crypto, AI, encrypted messaging, on-chain identity — every one of these stacks runs on jurisdictions that respect property, privacy, and contract. New Hampshire already does.

Digital Property

Bitcoin, NFTs, on-chain assets, self-custodied keys. Property under the law, full stop.

Open Networks

Public blockchains and end-to-end encryption are the rails. No CBDCs. No backdoors.

The Right to Build

Founders, miners, node operators, AI labs. Clear rules. A state that gets out of the way.

Issues Framework

Six pillars.
One scorecard.

Endorsements are issue-based. A candidate's score on these six decides whether they earn our support.

PILLAR / 01

Self-Custody

Hold your own keys. No bans, no licensing, no carve-outs.

PILLAR / 02

Zero Digital Tax

No state-level tax on crypto, mining, staking, or digital-asset gains.

PILLAR / 03

No CBDC

Block any central-bank digital currency from state legal tender or systems.

PILLAR / 04

Right to Mine

PILLAR / 05

Encryption

Recent Record

NH is moving.

The legislature has already taken concrete action. We're tracking and reinforcing it.

HB 302 / 2025

Strategic Bitcoin Reserve

NH was among the first states in the nation to authorize a state-level digital-asset reserve, allowing the Treasurer to hold Bitcoin and qualifying digital assets.

Long-standing

Zero State Capital Gains Tax

No state-level tax on capital gains. The Interest & Dividends tax was fully repealed in 2025. Every dollar of upside stays with the holder.

Civil Liberty / 2017

Constitutional Carry

NH's record on physical-world rights tells you what to expect on digital ones. The same legislature that protects self-defense will protect self-custody.

Universal / 2025

Education Freedom Accounts

NH led on letting families direct education funding. The same DNA — individual choice over state mandate — is what we apply to money, identity, and data.

Live free. Build free.

The same Granite State that wrote "Live Free or Die" into law is the right place to write the legal foundation of the next financial system.
