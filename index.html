Track Record

NH is already moving.

The legislature has already enacted — or is actively advancing — the policy stack the rest of the country is fighting for.

First in U.S. Signed · Ch. 4

HB 302 · 2025

Strategic Bitcoin Reserve

New Hampshire became the first state in the nation to authorize a state-level Strategic Bitcoin Reserve, allowing the Treasurer to hold up to 5% of public funds in qualifying digital assets and precious metals. Signed by Gov. Ayotte on May 6, 2025.

Bitcoin Magazine · CoinDesk
First in U.S. In effect

RSA 301-B:14 · Effective July 2025

NH DAO Act

NH became the first state to grant decentralized autonomous organizations legal personhood and limited liability through a permissionless-blockchain registry — without piggybacking on LLC law. Listings managed by the Secretary of State.

McLane Middleton · CoinDesk
Signed · Ch. 177

HB 310 · 2025

Stable Token & Tokenized RWA Commission

Established a study commission to draft NH's regulatory framework for stable tokens, tokenized real-world assets, and blockchain-based trusts. Signed July 15, 2025.

Passed House In Senate

HB 1124 · 2026

Right to Compute

Statutory protection of the right of New Hampshire residents to use computational resources without state interference. Cleared the House 3/11/2026 (Committee 15-0). Senate Judiciary heard 4/21/2026.

Passed both chambers

HB 1073 · 2026

DAO Registry Clarifier

Clarifies the timeline for the Secretary of State to complete the NH DAO Registry. House and Senate both passed without dissent (Senate Commerce 5-0).

Planned · First in U.S.

$100M Muni Bond · 2025-2026

Bitcoin-Backed Municipal Bond

NH is moving toward issuing what would be the first Bitcoin-backed municipal bond in the United States — approved in late 2025 and progressing through 2026 sign-off.

Yahoo Finance · NHPR
