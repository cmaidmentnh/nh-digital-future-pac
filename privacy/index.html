Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

What we collect

If you join the briefing list, we collect your email address, IP address (for fraud prevention), the page you signed up from, and any UTM parameters in the URL. If you contact the PAC, we receive whatever you send.

If you donate, we collect the information legally required for campaign-finance reporting under New Hampshire RSA 664: name, mailing address, occupation and employer (above the statutory threshold), amount, and date.

What we don't

We do not run third-party advertising trackers on this site. We do not sell, rent, or trade your email or contact information to other organizations. We do not require an account to read anything published here.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to measure aggregate site traffic. GA4 sets cookies and processes IP addresses on Google's infrastructure. You can opt out by using a browser-level tracker blocker; the site remains fully functional.

Donor disclosure

New Hampshire campaign-finance law requires PACs to itemize and report contributions above statutory thresholds. We comply with that law. Donor information reported to the Secretary of State becomes public record. Contributions below the disclosure threshold are aggregated and reported anonymously per the statute.

Cryptocurrency contributions

BTC contributions are recorded with the contributor's identifying information (collected at the donation form), the receiving address, transaction ID, USD value at spot at time of receipt, and date. The same disclosure rules apply as to USD contributions.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from them.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from any email at any time using the link in the email. You can request deletion of your contact record by emailing contact@digitalfuturenh.com. Contribution records that the PAC is required by law to retain cannot be deleted on request.

Contact

Questions: contact@digitalfuturenh.com
